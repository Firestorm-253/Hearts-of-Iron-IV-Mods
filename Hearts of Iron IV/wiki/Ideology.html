Democracy[edit]

Democracy is a political form in which popular vote govern directly or indirectly.

Traits[edit]

  • Can NOT declare war on country in same ideology group without wargoal
  • Can Guarantee other ideologies
  • Lowers World Tension with Guarantees
  • Can NOT puppet a country
  • Can NOT only justify wargoals against countries that have generated World Tension
  • Take states cost: +25%
  • Annex cost: +50%

Tension limits[edit]

  • Declare War limit: +100%
  • Join Faction tension limit: +80%
  • Lend-Lease tension limit: +50%
  • Send Volunteers tension limit: +50%
  • Guarantee world tension limit: +25%
