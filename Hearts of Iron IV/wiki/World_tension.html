World Tension at 100%

World Tension determines the level of tension that exists in the World, in a number between 1% and 100%. The level of World Tension has several impacts, particularly allowing/limiting political decisions, national foci, and more. It is shown as an icon depicting Earth, with flames swallowing it the higher current World Tension is. If events that reduce World Tension occur at a low percent, it can make Tension drop to less than that 1%.

Impacts on World Tension[edit]

Increase in World Tension[edit]

The following increases the level of World Tension:

  • Declaring war
  • Annexing
  • Sending volunteers
  • Joining a faction

Decrease in World Tension[edit]

The following causes a decrease in World Tension:

  • Decay over time
  • Peace treaties
  • Guaranteeing (Democracy)

Ideological effect[edit]

Democracies[edit]

Democracy is a political form in which popular vote govern directly or indirectly. World Tension limits:

  • Declare War limit: +100%
  • Join Faction tension limit: +80%
  • Lend-Lease tension limit: +50%
  • Send Volunteers tension limit: +50%
  • Guarantee world tension limit: +25%

Political decision's impact on World Tension by country[edit]

Germany[edit]

Increases World Tension:

  • Remilitarization of the Rhineland
  • Anschluss
  • Demand Sudetenland
  • First Vienna Award

  • Limited rearmament
