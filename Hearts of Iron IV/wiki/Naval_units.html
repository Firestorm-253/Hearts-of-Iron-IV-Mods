Variants[edit]

Naval units may be customized using naval experience. This consists of assigning points to four categories. The categories available varies with the type of class. Here are known categories:

  • Armor (Increases protection especially against air attacks but reduce speed and deckspace.)
  • Reliability (Reduces the chance of the ship getting critically hit.)
  • Weapons/Deckspace (Weapons: Unknown/Deckspace: Increases the max number of aircraft the ship can carry.)
  • Engine (Increases speed.)

Each point spent in non-reliability categories increases the chance of the ship getting critically hit.

Variants may be changed during production at a cost of some delay.

Unit types[edit]

Taken from the a tech screen[1].

  • Destroyer (DD)
  • Light cruiser (CL)
  • Heavy cruiser (CA)
  • Battlecruiser (BC)
  • Battleship (BB)
  • Super-heavy battleship (SHBB)
  • Aircraft carrier (CV)
  • Submarine (SS)
  • Transports (TP): Transports, landing craft, tank landing craft

At current there do not seem to be any light/escort carriers (CVL/CVE).
